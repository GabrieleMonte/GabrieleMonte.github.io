Research Intern

Oxford University- Summer Internship Program

My work centered on the development of a machine learning (ML) algorithm using partial convolutional neural networks (PCNNs) to in-paint masked images of the the cosmic microwave background (CMB). The network we developed can reconstruct both the maps and the power spectra to a few percent for circular and irregularly shaped masks covering up to ~10% of the image area.

Finally our model also outperforms the other recently developed ML methods that inpaint pure CMB maps. For more info click on the link below:
